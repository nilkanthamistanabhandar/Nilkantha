About Us

Nilkanto Mistanna Bhandar has been serving authentic Bengali sweets for over 30 years. Our commitment to quality, freshness, and traditional recipes has made us a trusted name in the sweet industry.

We use only the finest milk, ghee, and natural ingredients. Every sweet is carefully prepared by our experienced craftsmen to ensure the perfect taste and texture that our customers love.
